IRC - use services
==================

Examples :

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
/msg NickServ register <password> <email>

/msg chanserv register #mychannel

/msg operserv mode #mychannel +o louis

/msg operserv mode #mychannel +nt
/msg operserv mode #mychannel -i

/msg botserv assign #mychannel ChanServ

/msg OperServ stats
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
