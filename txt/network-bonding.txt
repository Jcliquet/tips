network - Linux network bonding (teaming)
=========================================

Install NICs on separate busses
-------------------------------

For availability.

Configure the module
--------------------

el5 : in `/etc/modprobe.conf`        

el6 : in `/etc/modprobe.d/bond.conf`

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
alias bond0 bonding
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Configure the interfaces
------------------------

In `/etc/sysconfig/network-scripts`

ifcfg-bond0
~~~~~~~~~~~

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
DEVICE=bond0
IPADDR=192.168.0.1
PREFIX=24
ONBOOT=yes
BOOTPROTO=none
BONDING_OPTS="mode=6 miimon=100 use_carrier=0"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ifcfg-eth0
~~~~~~~~~~

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
DEVICE=eth0
MASTER=bond0
SLAVE=yes
ONBOOT=yes
BOOTPROTO=none
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ifcfg-eth1
~~~~~~~~~~

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
DEVICE=eth1
MASTER=bond0
SLAVE=yes
ONBOOT=yes
BOOTPROTO=none
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
